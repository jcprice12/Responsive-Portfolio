About Me

Hi there! Welcome to my personal website. Thank you for being here.


I'm a software developer from the Charlotte, NC area who loves tackling challenging problems. Seriously, sometimes I forget to go to the bathroom when I'm nose-deep in my computer screen. Writing code is something that I love to do and I'm always interested in exploring exciting and new technology. I received my B.S. in Computer Science from Wake Forest University in May of 2015 and I've been learning new things ever since.


Although software development is fun and all, I do have other hobbies. I like reading, running, and breakdancing. If you ever see me dancing in public, it's not my twin brother (I promise).
